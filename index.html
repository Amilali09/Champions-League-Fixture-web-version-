Rules
Total 36 Teams participate in UEFA Champions League.
Total 144 Matches to be played (8 Matches per Team).
All 36 Teams are dividen into 4 pots of 9 teams each.
Each Team plays against exactly 2 teams from each pot.
Exactly 4 Home and 4 Away Games should be played per Team.
Top 8 Teams directly Qualify for Round of 16.
Teams ranking 9-24 play against each other for rest 8 spots.
No repeated Fixtures.
Teams from same Leagues will not play against each other eg.     "Barcelona vs Real Madrid" is not allowed.
